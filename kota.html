Welcome to Kota

Kota is a prominent city located in the southeastern part of the Indian state of Rajasthan, situated along the banks of the Chambal River. Known historically for its significance under Rajput rule and modern relevance as an education hub.

Kota is famous for its vibrant culture, historic palaces, and the scenic Chambal river. Known as the education hub of Rajasthan, Kota offers a blend of heritage and modernity.
Things to do:

  • Visit Garadia Mahadev Temple overlooking the Chambal River
  • Explore Seven Wonders Park with its unique architectural replicas
  • Discover Chambal Wildlife Sanctuary for bird watching and natural beauty
  • Visit historic Jag Mandir Palace on Kishore Sagar Lake
  • Enjoy scenic walks along the Chambal Riverfront

The city's unique blend of industrial growth and cultural heritage makes it an exciting place to explore for tourists and students alike.
